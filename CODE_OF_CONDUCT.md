# Code of conduct

Contributors must communicate professionally, respect privacy, use de-identified examples, and avoid stigmatizing language about disability, mental health, substance use, sexual health, military service, or benefits claims.

Harassment, doxxing, threats, fabricated evidence, pressure to expose medical details, or discriminatory conduct is not accepted. Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these standards. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository’s security or maintainer channels without sharing unnecessary personal information.
