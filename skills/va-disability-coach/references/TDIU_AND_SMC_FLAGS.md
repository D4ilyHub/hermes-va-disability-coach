# Separate flags: TDIU, SMC, temporary and protection issues

Do not add these issues automatically to the basic combined percentage.

- **TDIU:** flag when service-connected disability may prevent substantially gainful employment. Schedular thresholds and the economic/non-economic inquiry require separate analysis. [CFR-4.16] [VA-TDIU] [CASE-RAY] [CASE-RICE]
- **SMC:** flag loss or loss of use, specified combinations, aid and attendance, housebound questions, and anatomical/functional losses. SMC can be relevant even with a 100-percent evaluation. [CFR-3.350] [CFR-3.352] [VA-SMC]
- **Temporary evaluations:** flag qualifying hospitalization, surgery, cast, convalescence, or prestabilization. [CFR-4.28] [CFR-4.29] [CFR-4.30] [VA-TEMP] [VA-HOSPITAL]
- **Protection/reduction/severance:** flag duration, material improvement, examination adequacy, due process, and protected-service-connection/evaluation rules. [CFR-3.105] [CFR-3.343] [CFR-3.344] [CFR-3.951] [CFR-3.957]
- **Amputation and paired-organ rules:** flag, do not calculate in the basic engine. [CFR-4.68] [USC-1160]
- **Staged ratings/extraschedular:** flag distinct severity periods or unusual disability pictures for professional review. [CFR-3.321]

The skill should state the triggering facts and missing evidence without asserting entitlement.
