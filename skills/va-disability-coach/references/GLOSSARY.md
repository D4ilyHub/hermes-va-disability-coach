# Glossary

- **ACDUTRA / INACDUTRA:** statutory duty categories that can affect Guard/Reserve eligibility analysis. [USC-101] [CFR-3.6]
- **BDD:** Benefits Delivery at Discharge, an expedited pre-discharge claim process with a normal 180-to-90-day window. [VA-BDD]
- **C&P examination:** claim examination used to develop evidence; not ordinary treatment. [VA-C&P]
- **Claimed condition:** the benefit issue presented to VA; not automatically a medical diagnosis.
- **Current diagnosis:** a clinician’s diagnosis documented in evidence; distinct from symptoms and code selection.
- **Diagnostic code:** rating-schedule identifier used to evaluate a disability; not proof of service connection.
- **DBQ:** structured Disability Benefits Questionnaire used to capture examination evidence. [VA-DBQ]
- **Favorable finding:** a finding identified by VA that may have binding effect within the review system subject to applicable rules. [CFR-3.104]
- **Nexus evidence:** evidence addressing a relationship between disability and service or another disability; not synonymous with diagnosis.
- **Pyramiding:** duplicate evaluation of the same manifestation under multiple diagnoses. [CFR-4.14]
- **SHA:** Separation Health Assessment. [VA-SHA]
- **SMC:** special monthly compensation, analyzed separately from the basic combined percentage. [CFR-3.350]
- **TDIU:** total disability based on individual unemployability, a separate entitlement analysis. [CFR-4.16]
