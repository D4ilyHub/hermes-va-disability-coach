# Endocrine and metabolic

**Scope:** diabetes, thyroid, endocrine conditions, metabolic effects. A symptom is not a diagnosis, a diagnosis is not a service-connection theory, and neither automatically selects a diagnostic code.

## Documentation prompts

diagnosis; laboratory results; diet/medications/insulin as prescribed; regulation of activities only if medically required; episodes/hospitalization; complications; weight/energy changes. Preserve source and timeframe. Ask what is typical, what varies, and what the user tends to omit. Do not infer cause from location or temporal association.

## Clinical and rating boundaries

Seek medically appropriate evaluation. The skill may organize questions and documented findings but does not diagnose or direct treatment. Candidate rating criteria are reviewed only after diagnosis and provenance-bearing facts are supplied; qualitative standards remain manual. Check overlap with other systems before proposing separate evaluations.

## Urgent escalation

Stop claim work for: severe hypoglycemia, diabetic emergency signs, altered consciousness. Use `URGENT_MEDICAL.md` or `CRISIS_SAFETY.md` as appropriate.
