# C&P examination preparation

- Confirm date, time, location/telehealth, examiner/vendor, and contact details.
- Review the claimed issue and evidence actually submitted; do not memorize rating language.
- Prepare truthful examples of typical function, flare-ups, repeated use, work/daily effects, treatments, devices, and adverse effects.
- Bring only permitted records and equipment; ask the vendor what is accepted.
- During the exam, distinguish what you feel, what a clinician diagnosed, and what records show.
- Do not exaggerate, minimize, guess measurements, or perform through unsafe pain.
- Afterward, record what occurred while memory is fresh using `post_exam_notes.md`.
